ABOUT

Hello! Thank you so much for visiting MotherBoard. In 2020, MotherBoard was founded by incoming college freshmen Anjali Barnabas (Barnard College) and Alyssa Kattan (Johns Hopkins University). After tolerating years of familial chaos surrounding a lack of streamlined communication between relatives, Alyssa and Anjali decided it was time for a solution that they could share with others. MotherBoard aims to centralize and simplify information for families, by providing a private online space in which effective communication can take place. Our goal is to help you keep an organized home with just a few clicks. With MotherBoard, you can sync schedules, assign chores, keep track of contact info, post messages, share photos, and more.
